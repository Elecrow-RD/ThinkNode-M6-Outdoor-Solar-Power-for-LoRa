Thinknode M6 LoRaWAN Usage:

Brief Introduction to Firmware:

1. Supports AT commands for configuring network parameters, joining networks, and sending data;
2. Press the function key once to power on, hold it for 5 seconds to power off, double-click to join the network based on current parameters;
3. Upon power-on, the red indicator stays solid for 5 seconds then turns off. When powering off, the red indicator flashes twice at 3Hz before shutting down.
4. During operation, the red indicator flashes once every 5 minutes at 3Hz.
5. During charging, the red indicator flashes at 1Hz. When fully charged, it stays solid.
6. When the device has low battery, the red indicator flashes three times at a 3Hz frequency every 2 minutes;
7. When the device is powered on but not connected to the network, the blue indicator breathes;
8. Upon successful network connection, the blue indicator flashes three times at a 3Hz frequency. Upon failed connection, the red indicator flashes three times at a 3Hz frequency, and the blue indicator returns to breathing mode;
9. After successful network connection, the blue LED flashes twice at a 3Hz frequency when the LoRa module transmits data.

Copy the serial port tool to the desktop and double-click to open it.
